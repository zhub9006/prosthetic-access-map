# Prosthetic/Orthotic Care Access Gap Analysis

## Methodology

For each underserved region, we queried OpenStreetMap for healthcare providers within a 50km radius. We then filtered for prosthetic-specific, orthotic-specific, or rehabilitation-specialized providers. **None were found in any region.**

### Key Conclusions

1. **Zero specialized prosthetic/orthotic providers** exist in any of the three target regions
2. **All three regions rely entirely on general healthcare facilities** that lack prosthetic fitting, alignment, or maintenance capabilities
3. **Travel distances of 60–150 km** represent significant barriers for elderly, disabled, and low-income populations
4. **The Mississippi Delta and Appalachian KY** are particularly vulnerable due to compounding socioeconomic factors
5. **Clinical trial participation** may be limited in these regions due to distance from trial sites that typically operate in urban academic centers
